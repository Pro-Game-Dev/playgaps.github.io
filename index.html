Questions

Good to know before you install.

What ages are Play Gaps games designed for?

Every Play Gaps game is designed for kids ages 3 and up, with big buttons, simple taps, and no reading required to get started.

Are the games actually free?

Yes — all 16 games are free to download and free to start. A few ads keep the lights on, and any extra content is always optional, never required to play.

What does the Teacher Approved badge mean?

Teacher Approved is a Google Play program that recognizes high-quality apps for kids, reviewed against Google Play's own guidelines. Six Play Gaps games currently carry the badge — look for the ribbon on their cards.

Do you collect data from kids?

Data handling is disclosed per game in its Google Play Data Safety section — that's the most accurate, current place to check, and every card on this page links straight to it.

How do I actually install a game?

Tap any game card on this page. It opens that game's official Google Play listing in a new tab, where you install it the same way as any other Android app.
