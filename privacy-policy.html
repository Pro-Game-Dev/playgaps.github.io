Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version: Play Gaps games are built for kids, so we keep data collection to a minimum. Every game's exact, current data practices are always disclosed in that game's own Google Play Data Safety section — that's the most accurate place to check before you install.

Who we are

Play Gaps designs pre-education Android games for kids ages 3 and up — counting, the alphabet, shapes, colors, animals, and times tables. This policy explains, in plain language, how data is handled across our games and this website.

What this website collects

playgaps.com is a static informational site hosted on GitHub Pages. It doesn't have accounts, doesn't run analytics scripts that track you individually, and doesn't collect personal information beyond what your browser or GitHub Pages' hosting infrastructure logs automatically (like standard server access logs).

Tapping a game card takes you to that game's official Google Play listing — this site never handles any part of a game install itself.

What our games collect

Data practices can differ from game to game, since some show ads and some don't. Rather than repeat details here that could go out of date, every Play Gaps game lists its exact, current data handling in its Google Play Data Safety section, including:

  • What data (if any) is collected
  • Whether data is shared with third parties, such as ad networks
  • Whether data collection is optional
  • How data is secured

You can find this by opening any game's Play Store page and scrolling to the "Data safety" section — the same page you reach by tapping any card on our home page.

Advertising

Some Play Gaps games are free to play and supported by ads served through third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob). These networks may use device identifiers to serve and measure ads. Where a game targets children, ad serving follows Google Play's Families Policy requirements, including restrictions on personalized advertising where applicable.

Children's privacy

Our games are designed for young children, and we build them with that responsibility in mind. We don't knowingly collect more information than a game's stated purpose requires, and we don't ask children for personal details like names, addresses, or contact information to play.

Your choices

You can review or adjust ad personalization settings from your Android device's Google Settings at any time, and you can uninstall any Play Gaps game the same way you would any other app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our games or this website after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact us

Questions about privacy or data handling? Reach out any time at playgapsofficial@gmail.com.
