Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Looking for the policy for one of our children's educational games? Those are covered separately by our Kids Privacy Policy, which never permits personalized advertising. This page covers Play Gaps' general-audience apps and games instead — things like our platformers and puzzle games.

The short version

  • Every Play Gaps app tells you plainly, in its own store listing, whether it's built for everyone (including children) or only for teens and adults.
  • Apps built for everyone show only non-personalized, child-safe ads — same standard as our kids' games.
  • Apps meant only for teens/adults may show personalized ads. Where the law requires your permission first (EU, UK, Switzerland), we ask before turning them on, and you can change your mind anytime.
  • We don't require an account to play, and we never see your payment details — purchases go entirely through Google Play Billing.
  • You have real rights over your data — to know, correct, delete, or opt out — wherever you live.

Questions or a rights request? Email playgapsofficial@gmail.com — we read every message ourselves.

1. Who This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy applies to Play Gaps apps and games intended for a general audience — casual games such as platformers and puzzle games, and any future general-audience app we publish. It does not cover our children's educational games, which follow our separate Kids Privacy Policy.

Because our general-audience apps vary — some are suitable for everyone including children, while others are intended only for teens or adults — the specific practices below may differ from app to app. Each app's Play Store listing states plainly which category it falls into; this policy describes the full range of what's possible across our apps, not what every single app does.

2. Information We Collect

Not every Play Gaps app collects everything below — this is the complete range across our general-audience apps:

  • Advertising identifiers and limited device or usage information, processed by our advertising partners to serve ads
  • Basic technical information — device type, operating system version, general (non-precise) region, and crash or performance data — used to keep the app running properly
  • In-app purchase records, processed entirely through Google Play Billing
  • If a specific app offers optional features like leaderboards, achievements, or cloud saves through Google Play Games Services, an account identifier tied to those features

No Play Gaps app requires you to create an account just to play. Where a feature like cloud save is optional, you can use the app without it.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information above only to:

  • Serve ads — personalized or non-personalized, depending on the specific app and your consent choices
  • Process in-app purchases through Google Play Billing
  • Keep the app stable, secure, and free of bugs
  • Provide optional features you choose to use, like leaderboards or cloud saves

We do not sell your personal information for money. Depending on the app and where you live, sharing data with ad partners to serve ads can still count as a "sale" or "sharing" under certain laws (including California's) even without money changing hands — see Your Privacy Rights for how to opt out.

4. Advertising

Play Gaps apps fall into two advertising categories. Check the specific app's store listing to see which one applies:

🟢 Apps for everyone, including children

Ads are always non-personalized and contextual, tagged as child-directed with Google AdMob — the same standard used across our kids' games. No profiling, no behavioral targeting, ever, for anyone using these apps.

🟡 Apps for teens and adults only

Rated 13+ or higher, and genuinely built and marketed for that audience. These apps may show personalized ads based on your interests and activity. Where the law requires your permission first — including the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland — we show a consent screen, using a system certified by Google, before any personalized ad is served. You can change your choice anytime from the app's privacy settings.

All advertising runs through Google AdMob. If we ever add other ad networks through AdMob's mediation (for example, Unity Ads), we'll only use networks Google has approved for the relevant audience type, and we'll update this page to name them.

5. In-App Purchases

Some Play Gaps apps offer optional in-app purchases. Every purchase is processed entirely through Google Play's own billing system — we never receive, see, or store your payment card or financial information. If a specific app offers randomized virtual items (sometimes called "loot boxes"), the odds of receiving each item are disclosed in the app before you purchase, as required by Google Play policy.

6. Third-Party Services

Depending on the app, Play Gaps apps may use:

  • Google AdMob (advertising), including other ad networks used through AdMob's mediation, limited to networks Google has approved for the app's audience
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)
  • Google Play Games Services (optional leaderboards, achievements, and cloud saves, only in apps that offer these features)

We do not use these services to sell your personal information to unrelated third parties, and any provider we use is required by Google Play's policies to handle data securely rather than sell it onward.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Wherever you live, you generally have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we, or our service providers, hold about you, and why
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information, with reasonable exceptions (such as records needed to complete a purchase you've already made)
  • Opt out of the use of your data for personalized advertising, and of any "sale" or "sharing" of your data
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising any of these rights — we won't degrade your experience or change pricing because you opted out

If you're in the European Economic Area, the UK, or Switzerland, you also have the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Because these are mobile apps rather than websites, the most direct way to limit ad personalization is through your device's own settings (on Android: Settings → Google → Ads, or Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads personalization). We honor this device-level choice across our apps. If you ever reach a Play Gaps web page from a browser sending a Global Privacy Control signal, we honor that too, for anything processed through that page.

To exercise any of these rights, email playgapsofficial@gmail.com. Play Gaps is a small, independently-run studio, so you'll be reaching the developer directly — we aim to respond within the timeframe required by applicable law, typically 30–45 days.

8. Children and This App

Play Gaps' general-audience apps rated 13+ or higher are not directed to, or intended for use by, children under that age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the relevant age through these apps. If we learn that a child has provided us with personal information through one of these apps, we will delete it promptly.

If you're a parent and believe this has happened, contact us at playgapsofficial@gmail.com and we'll investigate and delete it. Apps specifically intended for young children are covered by our separate Kids Privacy Policy, which never permits personalized advertising regardless of age.

9. International Data Transfers

Play Gaps is based in India. Your information may be processed by our service providers — primarily Google — on servers located in the United States, the European Union, or elsewhere. Google maintains its own safeguards for these transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses where required under EU or UK law. We do not operate our own servers that store personal information.

10. Data Retention

We do not maintain our own database of personal information. Advertising and purchase data described in this policy is retained according to Google's own retention practices — see Google's Privacy Policy for details. If a specific app offers an account-based feature like cloud saves through Google Play Games Services, that data is governed by Google's own account and retention policies.

11. Security

We rely on Google's infrastructure — Play Billing, AdMob, Play Games Services — for anything involving payment or advertising data, which maintains its own industry-standard security measures. We don't operate a separate server or database of personal information for these apps, which limits our own exposure by design, not just by promise.

12. Changes to This Policy

If we make a meaningful change — for instance, if a new app or feature changes what information is processed — we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and post the revised policy here. We recommend checking back occasionally.

13. Contact Us

Questions, concerns, or a privacy rights request for any Play Gaps general-audience app:

Email: playgapsofficial@gmail.com

We aim to respond within a few business days.
